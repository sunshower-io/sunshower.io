What Can Sunshower.io's Anvil Optimization Do For Your Company?

Are you worried your AWS bill is too darn high? Are your servers crashing and timing out, and you’re not sure why things keep falling over? Sunshower.io can help you with these problems by optimizing the workloads you have running on AWS’s EC2 (Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud).

Our Anvil optimizer works by looking at the CloudWatch logs of your EC2 instances, crunching numbers across a variety of dimensions, and giving suggestions on the best fitting instance (or instances) for your different workloads. In many cases, the new instances will be less expensive because much of the hardware you’ve been paying for is underutilized. If crashing and timing out is your issue, we can identify situations where you might be running out of memory and recommend a better fit for your workload.

If you’re already running CloudWatch, Anvil can get to work crafting a solution right away. If not, you'll need to have CloudWatch on your system for about a week (depending upon when your peak activity is) for us to do our magic. Once we’ve ingested and analyzed your data, we’ll be able to craft the best possible solution for the cloud problems you’ve been facing.

Best of all, we only charge you if we save you money. We also only need limited, programmatic access to your systems run the Anvil optimization, so there's no need to hand over the keys to the kingdom to let us do our thing.

If these cloud pain points sound familiar to you but you’re not on AWS, no worries! We’re in the process of developing plugins for most major clouds. Let us know what cloud you're doing your computing on, and we'll make it a priority to put yours in the pipeline first.
