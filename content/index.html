In General

We want to help you make sense of the behemoth that is “the cloud” — and all of its permutations — with our plug-ins and open-source software. Whether you’re getting started, scaling up or trying to keep your tech rolling along, we’ve got you, fam.

  • Open and honest — in all things
  • Friendly — communicate technical concepts in clear language
  • Simple but powerful — to use and to develop on
  • Heckin useful — out-of-the-box and with plug-ins, from functionality to help documentation

The Details

  • Who We Are

    a tiny team that knows how much software and all the devops associated with it can suck

  • What We Do

    eliminate hassle, confusion and frustration with all things cloud by providing SDKs, UIs and, most importantly, clarity.

  • How We Do It

    a powerful plug-in framework geared towards simplifying cloud deployments, from the initial roll-out to transferring cloud service providers to optimizing existing installations.

Use Cases

Who are you? Stop having an existential crisis, and help us help you.

  • Single User
    • Start your project on the right foot with informed decisions about clouds and cloud infrastructure
    • Set yourself up for success by being cloud-agnostic
    • Use the same stuff we build our applications with to speed up basically anything you might be building
  • Scaling Startup
    • Make meaningful decisions about your cloud strategy based on insights about your workload
    • Easily scale within and across clouds
    • Tackle the problems you haven’t even run into yet by using others’ plug-ins or rolling your own
  • Enterprise Company
    • Use our SaaS offering or manage your own Sunshower installation to help you wrangle your customers’ infrastructure needs
    • Easily create plug-ins to address specific pain points in your organization
    • Make sense of different environments and VPCs
    • Handle the next “heartbleed” or “meltdown” in a few minutes and clicks, instead of a few hours, days or weeks
